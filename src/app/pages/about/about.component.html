#PositiveTourism

To us, positive tourism is a way of travelling with a mindfulness to respect, learn and grow from the local traditions and communities that you travel to. It intertwines travel with an indepth cultural experience that is interactive and hands-on.

Not only does positive tourism ensures cross-cultural understanding and mutual respect, this type of travelling makes sure that the local community will directly benefit from the economic benefits of tourism. By using traditional transportation, locals as guides, and local accommodation, money is always put back into the pockets of the locals. This creates jobs, redistribution of income and decreases poverty.

On a larger scale, this paves the way for increased community spending on important infrastructure projects such as roads, schools, sanitation and environmental sustainability. #Mission

Our mission, is simply, to use positive tourism to inspire a movement of global citizenship.

We believe that a global citizen is someone who identifies themselves as being a member of a single, borderless global community and wants to have an active role in building its values to benefit everyone. Positive tourism connects travellers and locals on a more personal level, quickly closing the cultural gaps between them. Travellers gain a unique understanding about the daily lives of locals, and the hardships they face. We believe by seeing this first-hand, prejudices about culture, religion, race and nationality disappear. Instead, empathy, admiration and gratitude are felt for those we meet while traveling, and as well, for their culture and the country they live in.

For this reason, positive tourism must be used as an important tool to educate ourselves and others on how we can better contribute to our global community. It is our hope, that this gained perceptive will transcend globally to inspire universal cooperation and compassion that will make the world a better place to live in for all of us. In this way, we strongly believe that positive tourism can create effective global citizens and be a major driver of world peace.
